Meet Duo Duo

Duo Duo is a 6 years old long-hair Ragamuffin who is house-trained. She loves sleeping, finding new sleeping spots, and getting pets. Duo Duo is overweight but her family is trying to help her exercise more.


Duo Duo can be in a household with both adults and children. She prefers a quiet environment with lots of hiding spots. Her favorite toys are hair ties, bouncy balls, and catnip toys.
